14/// \brief Represents a single sound object, either buffered or streamed.
15///
16/// CN3SndObj manages a single sound instance, including playback, looping,
17/// volume, 3D position, and lifetime of the underlying audio asset. It interacts
18/// with the sound manager to queue OpenAL operations on a dedicated audio thread.
19class CN3SndObj

21protected:

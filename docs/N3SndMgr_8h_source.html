236 /// \brief Releases the manager's reference to an audio asset.

241 /// This should be paired with a previous call to \c LoadBufferedAudioAsset or
242 /// \c LoadStreamedAudioAsset to ensure consistent lifetime management.
243 ///
244 /// \param audioAsset Pointer to the audio asset to release.

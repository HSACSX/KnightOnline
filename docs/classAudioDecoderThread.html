Detailed Description

Background thread responsible for decoding streamed audio data.

AudioDecoderThread runs continuously and incrementally decodes audio data for active streamed audio handles. Decoded PCM chunks are queued on each handle and consumed by the audio playback system.

Supported decoder types are MP3 and raw PCM streams.
